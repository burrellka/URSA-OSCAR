# Code of Conduct

URSA-OSCAR is a small project. The standards here are simple:

## Expected

- Be respectful in issues, PR reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good intent. Ask clarifying questions before reacting.
- Critique the code, not the contributor.
- When you disagree, say so clearly and explain why.

## Not tolerated

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Deliberately disruptive or off-topic posts on issues / PRs.
- Sharing or asking for any other contributor's personal health data, even in the form of "show me your AHI for X night."

## Enforcement

The repository maintainer ([@burrellka](https://github.com/burrellka)) handles enforcement. Possible outcomes for violations include:
- A warning, public or private.
- Removal of contributions.
- A temporary or permanent ban from the repository.

Decisions are at the maintainer's discretion and are final.

## Reporting

For violations of this policy, contact the maintainer via the email associated with their GitHub account, or open a private security advisory (which is also the channel for general private-disclosure matters per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)).
